About Us

What started as a small idea way back in 2012 has now grown multifold. With a diverse and flourishing business invloving 151 employees spread across the length and breadth of the country, Triportal is visited by more than 50,000 unique visitors every week. In response to this overwhelming support, the company has expanded rapidly, now catering to more than 10,000 queries every week. The company now covers all the major off-beat places that provide the invaluable lifetime experience.

Vision

The company aims to become one-stop destination for solo travellers who are looking for well-planned, cost-effective and guided trips. Triportal majorly focuses on the need and requirements of the solo travellers, encouraging and preparing them to plan and execute their solo trips themselves. The company also envisions to provide a sense of community, a platform for networking and most importantly a sense of belongingness to its members coming from diverse backgrounds, be it professional or cultural.
